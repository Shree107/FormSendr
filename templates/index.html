How FormSendr Works

Get started in minutes with just a few simple steps

1

Update Your Form

Change your form's action to point to FormSendr with your email as a parameter.

contact-form.html
<!-- Update your form action --> 
<form action="https://formsendr.onrender.com/send/your-email@example.com" method="POST">
  <input type="text" name="name" placeholder="Your Name" required>
  <input type="email" name="email" placeholder="Your Email" required>
  <textarea name="message" placeholder="Your Message" required></textarea>
  <button type="submit">Send Message</button>
</form>
